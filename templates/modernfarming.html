Aeroponic Method of Farming

Aeroponics is the process of growing plants in an air or mist environment without using soil. It works by suspending plant roots in the air and misting them with nutrient water.

    Advantages:

  • Ease of working with plants
  • Cost effective as you won't need irrigation channels
  • Disease-free cultivation due to minimal plant-to-plant contact

Hydroponic Method of Farming

Hydroponics is a soil-less farming method that uses water as the growing medium enriched with nutrients. It allows better plant growth, saves space and increases control.

    Advantages:

  • Reduces competition for nutrients, sunlight, and space
  • Helps control weed and pests
  • Reduces machinery cost
  • Maximizes profits from high-value crops

Hybrid Seed Technology

Hybrid seeds are formed by crossing two unrelated inbred plants to gain desired traits. They are commonly used in commercial farming to increase yield and resilience.

    Advantages:

  • Vigorous and uniform growth
  • Improved disease resistance
  • Higher yields
  • Better taste and shelf life
